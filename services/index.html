Our Service Solutions

From installation and commissioning to 24×7 breakdown support and calibration — we support your medical equipment through every stage of its lifecycle.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Support Services

Every service we offer is designed to keep your medical equipment performing at its best — protecting your patients and your accreditation.

Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

Our structured AMC plans provide scheduled preventive maintenance, calibration, and unlimited breakdown support — all under a single, transparent contract. We offer both Comprehensive (CMC) and Non-Comprehensive (AMC) plans.

  • Scheduled quarterly preventive maintenance visits
  • All labour charges covered
  • Priority emergency call response
  • Periodic calibration and performance verification
  • Documentation for NABH / NABL compliance

Installation & Commissioning

Our certified engineers handle the full installation lifecycle — from site survey and preparation through to equipment commissioning, acceptance testing, and handover with complete documentation.

  • Pre-installation site survey and requirements report
  • Professional equipment installation and integration
  • Acceptance and performance testing (FAT/SAT)
  • Handover documentation and user manuals
  • Warranty registration and OEM liaison

Calibration & Quality Control

We provide NABL-traceable calibration for all categories of biomedical equipment, generating detailed calibration certificates that meet NABH, JCI, and NABL requirements.

  • NABL-traceable calibration certificates
  • All types of biomedical equipment covered
  • Calibration scheduling and reminder management
  • NABH audit documentation support
  • Asset register maintenance and tracking

24×7 Breakdown Support

Our dedicated helpdesk is available around the clock. A single call connects you to our on-call engineers who can resolve most issues remotely — and dispatch on-site within 4 hours for critical care equipment.

  • 24×7 telephone and remote support helpdesk
  • 4-hour on-site emergency response guarantee
  • Dedicated account manager for AMC clients
  • Service call logging and reporting system
  • Loaner equipment provision on request

Training & Clinical Application Support

We provide structured user training for nursing staff, biomedical engineers, and clinical teams — covering equipment operation, alarms management, and routine checks for every device we supply.

  • On-site user training at time of installation
  • In-service education for new staff
  • Clinical application specialist support
  • Biomedical engineering team training
  • Training completion certificates provided

Hospital Project Consultancy & Procurement

Planning a new ward, ICU, or OT? Our consultants work with hospital architects, administrators, and clinical teams to design the optimal equipment layout and sourcing strategy for your project.

  • New hospital and department equipment planning
  • Equipment specifications and tender documents
  • Government / CMSS / DPIIT tender assistance
  • Budget planning and phased procurement
  • Turnkey project delivery and commissioning
