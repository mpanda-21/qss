About QSS Healthcare

Years of empowering healthcare facilities with world-class medical equipment and unwavering service commitment from Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

Our Journey

Built on Trust & Technology

QSS Healthcare — Quick Support System — was founded with a singular purpose: to ensure that no healthcare facility in India ever has to worry about equipment failure. Headquartered at STONEDGE TOWER, Ashok Nagar, Chennai, we have grown from a local service partner into a comprehensive medical equipment solutions company serving hospitals, nursing homes, and diagnostic centres.

We believe great healthcare starts with reliable equipment. That's why every product we supply comes with the promise of our team behind it.
