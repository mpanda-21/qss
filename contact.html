Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How quickly can you respond to a breakdown call?

For critical care equipment under our AMC, we guarantee on-site response within 4 hours. For non-urgent calls, we typically attend within 24 hours. Our helpdesk is available 24×7 for remote troubleshooting.

Do you service equipment from other suppliers?

Yes. We service and maintain equipment regardless of whether it was supplied by us, as long as we are authorized or trained for that make and model. Contact us with the equipment details for a serviceability check.

Can you help with NABH accreditation documentation?

Absolutely. Our calibration certificates are NABL-traceable and formatted for NABH audit requirements. We also assist with biomedical equipment inventory and maintenance record documentation.

Do you supply to government hospitals and tenders?

Yes. We regularly supply to government hospitals, medical colleges, and ESI institutions across Tamil Nadu. We assist with DPIIT, GeM, and CMSS tender processes. Contact us for details.

What areas do you cover for service support?

We primarily serve Chennai and Tamil Nadu, with service capability extending across South India. For locations outside our direct service area, we coordinate with OEM-authorized service partners.

Can you provide a product demo at our hospital?

Yes, we offer live product demonstrations at your facility for any equipment in our portfolio. Fill the contact form above requesting a demo and our clinical application specialist will be in touch within 48 hours.
